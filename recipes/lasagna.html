Lasagna

Description

Mmm, nothing like some home-cooked lasagna, just like your mama used to make. The layers of cheese, meat and sauce create a hearty dinner your family is sure to love.

Making a lasagna may seem like an intimidating process, but don't worry; if you follow these easy instructions you'll be in Italian heaven in no time. Enjoy!

Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 2 tsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb. ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3 oz tomato paste
  • 1 (14 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp. chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp. red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste, optional)

To assemble the lasagna

  • 1/2 lb. dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 lb mozarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1/4 pound freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions and garlic
  4. Add back the beef
  5. Make the sauce
  6. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  7. Preheat oven to 375 F
  8. Assemble the lasagna by layering noodles, sauce and cheese
  9. Bake for 45 minutes
  10. Cool and serve
