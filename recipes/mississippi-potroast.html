Mississippi Pot Roast

Description

Mississippi Pot Roast is a southern favorite that combines chuck roast, ranch dressing mix, au jus mix, butter and pepperocini peppers. That's it! Low on ingredients, high on flavor.

To make things even easier, it's slow cooked in a corckpot for several hours. This makes meal prep a breeze, while creating that melt-in-your-mouth tenderness.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 lb) chuck roast
  • 1/2 (12 oz) jar pepperoncinis
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 (1-oz) packet au jus gravy mis
  • 1 packet buttermilk ranch dressing mix
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a slow cooker, combine the chuck roast, pepperoncini, pepperoncini juice, butter, au jus gravy mix, buttermilk ranch dressing, salt and pepper
  2. Cook on low until the roast is fork-tender, about 8 hours
  3. Pull the chark apart with two forks. Enjoy!
